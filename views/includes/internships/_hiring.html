Who should apply?

Every designer starts out differently. We don’t require a specific background or experience level, but there’re a few things you should be be confident in:

  • Your graphic and visual design skills
  • Your ability to communicate or sell your process and ideas
  • Your portfolio
  • Your familiarity with common design tools—like Adobe CS, Sketch, Invision, and Figma.

Responsibilities

  • Join design team critiques
  • Sit in on client standups and discussions
  • Identify and research problems
  • Explore, build, and test concepts
  • Present your ideas, get feedback, and iterate
  • Take an internal project from concept to completion
